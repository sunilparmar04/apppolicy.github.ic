My mobile applications comply with the Childrens Online Privacy Protection Act. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, and if in the event that a user identifies himself or herself as a child under the age of 13 through a support request, I will not collect, store or use, and will delete in a secure manner, any personal information of such user.

I do not use or collect your precise geographic location.
